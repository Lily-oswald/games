Card Guessing Game

Try to guess the color, rank, and suit of the cards drawn from the deck!

How to Play:

  1. Red or Black: Guess if the next card will be red or black.
  2. Higher or Lower: Guess if the next card will be higher or lower than the first card.
  3. In Between or Outside: Guess if the third card's rank will be in between or outside the first two cards.
  4. Guess the Suit: Guess which suit the next card will be.

Complete all 4 steps to win!
